## "Could I hand this to 5 design partners next week?"
**Yes — via concierge onboarding — with eyes open.** A partner can self-navigate understanding, setup, review, approval, and undo with zero docs. The remaining gate (running the AI workflow) is exactly what the concierge session + the partner's own agent cover; for the AI-forward persona (#4) it's near-frictionless.

## Remaining blockers (honest, out of 5C scope)
1. **F1 — AI screens flag-OFF + external-agent requirement + no in-admin human "do-the-work" path.** The dominant blocker. Fix = a *product/validation* program: enable a vertical slice (alt-text/SEO) behind a safe default and/or build a human action path. **Not a copy fix; not done here.**
2. **Operating a third-party assistant app** (Claude desktop) is inherently outside WPCC — concierge/video bridges it.
3. **WooCommerce/Elementor dormant on prod** — gates persona #5; postpone per PMF Discovery.
4. **AI value unproven in production** (key unset) — validation, not UX.
5. **Plaintext-option key at rest** (masked; encrypted storage is schema-bearing — STOP).

## Verdict
**Design-partner readiness V2: 7.5/10** for concierge onboarding (up from ~4 pre-5A).
